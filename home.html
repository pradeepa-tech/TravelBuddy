About Us

We created this not only because we are programmers, but also travelers at heart. We realized there was no easy place for travlers like us to get the most updated information on our destinations. So we seeked out to change this.

Using open source software and publicly available data, we created travel buddy to give travelers the information they need in one place but better. Our website updates automatically as soon as there is any change so you can rest assured the information you are getting is recent.
